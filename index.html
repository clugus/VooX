What We Do

Learn to Speak

Practice speaking with someone who is fluent in the language you are trying to learn.

Practice In Real Time

Someone will be available to practice anytime, anywhere. No more waiting for pen-pals or trying to coordinate schedules with tutors.

Earn Points

Earn points by helping others practice speaking in the language you are fluent in. Points can be redeemed for gifts or money.

Speaking Made Easy, Go from Novice to Native

Learn

Practice speaking the language you are trying to learn with someone who is fluent in that language. Each new user is initially given a set amount of points to give to their teacher. Want to earn more points? Help someone else practice speaking.

Teach

Help someone practice speaking in the language you are fluent in. Earn points for your time teaching and use them to practice in the language you are trying to learn. Or continue to earn by teaching and redeem your points for gifts or money!
